Help With Hockey

Positions

There are many different positions in hockey such as offence, defence, center, and goalie.

Offence - The main goal of this position is to shoot the puck into the net.

Defence - The main goal of this position is to stop the other team from shooting on the goalie

Goalie - The main goal of this position is to stay at the net and protect it from any incoming shots

Center - This position is a mixture of offence and defence.

How to play

Hockey is played with a puck (A flat, circular piece of rubber). The goal of the game is to score more points than the other team which is accomplished by putting the puck in the net. Depending on the score (If it is tied or not) the game may go into overtime. This means that the game is prolonged in order to find a winner of the game.
